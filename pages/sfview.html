Commerce Cloud Demo Builder
Welcome to the Commerce Cloud Demo Builder, to start enter the project details and URL to a PLP of the site you wish to extract products from.
We now need to identify all the links to the PDP pages, you can hover over the products to identify the link. Once identified Right Mouse click to select. Keep selecting further products till we can identify the slector pattern.
 
Navigate to a PDP, from here we can extract product data, and map it to the Commerce Cloud Product Model, once loaded click 'Next' to continue
Right click on the data you want to capture, select the field to map it to and the data type, and click add. Continue till you have added all the fields you want to collect, then click next.
